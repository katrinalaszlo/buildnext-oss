# No Per-Customer Margin Visibility

**Status:** proposed
**Type:** new_feature
**Evidence:** 6 customers

## User Story

> As a B2B AI founder I want to see margins broken down by customer and feature so that I can identify which accounts are profitable and which are underwater.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Dashboard shows margin per customer with cost and revenue breakdown
- [ ] Drill-down from customer to feature-level profitability
- [ ] Costs auto-enriched from LLM provider public pricing
- [ ] Override option for negotiated/custom pricing tiers
