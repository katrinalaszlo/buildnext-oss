# Cost Intelligence

Per-customer margin visibility for AI-native SaaS companies.

**Stories:** 1 | **Evidence:** 15 quotes across 3 customers

---

## The problem

AI companies can't see their margins. Revenue lives in Stripe, costs live in OpenAI/Anthropic dashboards, and nobody connects them per customer. Founders know their aggregate margins but can't tell which customers are profitable and which are burning cash. Heavy users hide in the average.

The result: pricing decisions based on gut feelings, unprofitable customers subsidized by profitable ones, and CFOs who can't get unit economics by customer tier.

## Strongest signal

> "We had a customer generating 50,000 words a day. Our average is about 2,000. We didn't notice for three weeks. By the time our CTO flagged it, they'd burned through $4,000 in API costs on a $99 subscription." — [[Customer A]]

> "Our top-tier customers get 100x the value of our smallest customers but only pay 3x more. And I can't prove that to my board because I can't show per-customer unit economics." — [[Customer C]]
